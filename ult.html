Why do I like to write Poetries?

Writing has become my escape, to a world where there is no judgement and I could write the most ridiculous and ironic sentences ever and I would still be understood. It became my medium of release, whenever my thoughts gets too overwhelming for me, I can just let myself drown in the words I've written. I can write a tale of an adventurous journey of a character who braved the unknown. And I can let myself be transparent and vulnerable. Writing provides a void space for me, where I can reflect and know myself some more. It also allows me to explore and challenge my creativity.
When I write, I do not just write simple words, but I take my heart out and feel my soul. I close my eyes and I try to feel what the right words are, I devote my entirety when I write. I let myself be honest and weak, in writing I can fall, and drown, and break and I'd find comfort in that. Because sometimes, accepting that I am suffering and hurting is comforting for me, so I can release the pain I'm feeling through the words and just let the pen bleed ink. Additionally, in this alternate world, I can shapeshift into anything I want to be. I can be as imaginative or bland, be a hero or the villian. Furthermore, writing allows me to regulate my emotions and gather my thoughts, it gives me the satisfaction that I need and it's just pure bliss for me. It's a field where I reign, a place where there is no losing.
